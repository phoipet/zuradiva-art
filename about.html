About Me

A Balinese artist dedicated to transforming spaces through the timeless beauty of paintings, murals, and reliefs.

Rooted in the rich artistic heritage of Bali, my work blends traditional craftsmanship with modern inspiration. From vibrant wall murals to intricate relief carvings and fine art on canvas, each piece is handcrafted to evoke emotion, energy, and meaning.

With over a decade of experience in creating custom artworks for both private and commercial spaces, I specialize in bringing unique artistic visions to life. My work is deeply influenced by Balinese culture, nature, and contemporary artistic movements, creating pieces that bridge the past and present.

Whether you're looking to transform a residential space, create a distinctive atmosphere for your business, or commission a unique piece of art, I'm here to collaborate and bring your vision to life.
